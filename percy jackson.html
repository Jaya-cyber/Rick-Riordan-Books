Books 1-5

The Percy Jackson series, written by Rick Riordan, follows the adventures of Percy Jackson, a teenage boy who discovers he is a demigod, the son of Poseidon. Throughout the series, Percy navigates a world where Greek mythology is real, encountering gods, monsters, and other mythical beings. Alongside his friends Annabeth Chase and Grover Underwood, Percy embarks on quests to prevent catastrophic events, often finding himself at the center of prophecies that shape the fate of both mortals and gods. The series blends humor, action, and elements of classical mythology into a modern-day setting, appealing to readers of all ages with its mix of thrilling adventures and relatable characters.

Book 6-9
