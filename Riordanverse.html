best reading order

This is my personal thoughts on how to read these books, you are not obligated to follow them, but I've read these books so many times at this point that I know what I'm talking about...so you know, take it from an experienced Riordanverse reader. And I'll write the canon timeline order as well.

My Reading Order

  1. Percy Jackson and the Olympians
  2. The Heroes of Olympus
  3. Trials of Apollo
  4. The Sun and the Star
  5. The Kane Chronicles
  6. Magnus Chase and the Gods of Asgard

    7. Theres also all the companion books, but they don't really affect how you read the series.
